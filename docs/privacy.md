# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [Date]

This Privacy Policy explains how NIRA-X-Guardian ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) and Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Account Data**: Email address, encrypted password.
- **Profile Data**: Family names, child profile names (optional).
- **Usage Data**: DNS queries (domains visited), timestamps, client IP address, device identifiers.
- **Policy Data**: Blocklists, whitelists, category settings.

## 2. Purpose of Processing

We process your data to:
- Provide parental control and content filtering services.
- Enforce your family's safety policies.
- Generate activity reports for parents.
- Improve service security and performance.

**Legal Basis**: Performance of Contract (providing the service) and Legitimate Interest (security, child safety).

## 3. Data Retention

- **DNS Logs**: Retained for 7 days, then automatically deleted.
- **Account Data**: Retained until account deletion.

## 4. Data Security

We implement technical measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS).
- Hashing of passwords (Bcrypt).
- Access controls and minimal privilege for administrators.

## 5. Your Rights (NDPA/NDPR)

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data.
- **Rectify**: Correct inaccurate data.
- **Erase**: Request deletion of your account and data ("Right to be Forgotten").
- **Restrict**: Limit how we process your data.
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format.

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at: [dpo@example.com]

## 6. Consent

By using NIRA-X-Guardian, you consent to the processing of your network traffic for the purpose of content filtering. You represent that you have the authority to consent for all devices and minors on your network.
